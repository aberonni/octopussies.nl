Nothing is beyond our reach

Once upon a time there was a group of improvisers that consistently blew the mind of their audiences. Throughout the Amsterdam improv scene, they were known as the Octopussies. Those who watched their shows were imbued with a sense of magic and wonder; a newfound understanding of comedy and the reality that we live in every day; a sense of peace and oneness with the universe and all of its creations. Who knew that improv comedy could have such profound implications? Who knew that hairbrush puns could be so funny?

If you have been unfortunate enough not to witness this magic firsthand, fear not. The Octopussies shall one day return to their rightful home: the stage. It is there thay they belong, and it is there that they shall blow your minds. We shall conquer the stage once again and remind the audience that nothing is beyond our reach.
